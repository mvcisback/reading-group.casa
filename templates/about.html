About

Reading Group Coordinator

This tool keeps nominations, votes, and the schedule coordinated through FastAPI, HTMX, and a SQLite backend.

Multi-page layout

The navigation separates scheduling, voting, and account pages while CSS view transitions keep page changes feeling fluid.

How it works

  • Nominate papers on the home schedule so the backlog stays stocked.
  • Vote once per paper once you are logged in on the dedicated vote screen.
  • Select papers for upcoming meetings with optional dates to keep the group on track.

Under the hood

FastAPI handles the POST hooks while HTMX keeps interactions snappy. New views still reuse shared partials for consistent styling.
